LBS ENTRANCE EXAM

In 2002 the Government of Kerala entrusted the conduct of State Eligibility Test with the LBS Centre for Science and Technology. State Eligibility Test is a prerequisite for recruitment of teachers in Higher Secondary Schools in Kerala. There shall be two papers for the SET examination. From 2024 onwards, nursing aspirants seeking admission to the B.Sc. Nursing courses in Kerala should appear for an entrance exam conducted by the LBS Centre for Science and Technology. Kerala B.Sc. Nursing is a four-year undergraduate program for students aiming to build up a career in nursing.. KERALA MCA entrance examination is the common entrance test conducted by the Commissioner of Entrance Examinations, Kerala, for admission to the MCA course offered by reputed universities / colleges in Kerala. Paper Pattern: 150 multiple choice questions with four options. Two hours duration. The application process for Kerala SET is entirely online, and interested candidates can apply via the official websites: LBS Centre Official Website : https://lbsedp.lbscentre.in/setjan25/ Kerala SET Application Portal : https://lbsedp.lbscentre.in/setjan25/online.php.
